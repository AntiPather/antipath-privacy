Privacy Policy

AntiPath  ·  Effective date: June 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 18, 2026

This policy is written in plain language. The short version: AntiPath does not collect, sell, or share your personal information. Everything it stores stays on your device.

1. Who we are

AntiPath is a mobile application that helps young people discover international opportunities, fellowships, scholarships, free courses, and ways to earn income. It is operated by Alexander Taylor, based in Hyattsville, Maryland, USA. You can reach us at Antipather@proton.me.

2. What data we collect

We collect no personal information. AntiPath does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

The only information stored by the app is saved locally on your device:

  • Bookmarks — opportunities, scholarships, or courses you save. Stored only on your device using your phone's local storage. We never see or receive this data.
  • Display preference — whether you have enabled night mode. Stored only on your device.

This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

3. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Payment information — AntiPath is a paid app purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and Google. AntiPath never sees, receives, or stores any payment details.

4. External links

AntiPath displays listings that link directly to third-party websites — including scholarship programs, fellowship organizations, course providers, and freelance platforms. When you tap a link, you leave AntiPath and visit that third party's website or app.

We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting any personal information to them.

5. Children's privacy

AntiPath is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from any user, no special handling is required. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Antipather@proton.me and we will address your concerns promptly.

6. Third-party services

The app does not currently integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store; Apple and Google may collect standard platform-level data (such as crash logs or download statistics) subject to their own privacy policies.

7. Data security

Since no personal data is transmitted to or stored by us, there is no personal data of yours at risk on our end. The data stored on your device (bookmarks and display preference) is protected by your device's own security model.

8. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, most data-rights requests (access, deletion, portability) do not apply. However:

  • You can delete your bookmarks and preferences at any time by uninstalling the app.
  • If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California and have questions about your rights, please contact us at Antipather@proton.me.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy — for example, if a future version of AntiPath adds new features that involve data collection — we will update the effective date above and, where required by law, notify you through the app or the store listing. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
