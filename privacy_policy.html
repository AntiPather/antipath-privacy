6. Antipather

Antipather is an on-device, guided-selection tool available to Globetrotter subscribers. It is not an artificial intelligence system and is not a cloud-based service. Antipather operates as a local decision tree entirely on your device, matching your selected answers to a pre-curated set of program recommendations.

The answers you select in Antipather are processed only on your device and are not transmitted to AntiPath, stored on our servers, or shared with any third party. When you tap a recommended link, that item is saved to your device’s local Saved section in the same way as any other bookmark; you can remove it at any time.
