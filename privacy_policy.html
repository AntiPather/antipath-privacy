Privacy Policy

AntiPath  ·  Effective date: June 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 1, 2026

This policy is written in plain language. The short version: AntiPath works without an account, and everything it stores stays on your device. If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google to sync across devices, we use your account identifier and email only to link and sync your data. We never sell or share your personal information.

1. Who we are

AntiPath is a mobile application that helps young people discover international opportunities, fellowships, scholarships, free courses, and ways to earn income. It is operated by Alexander Taylor, based in Hyattsville, Maryland, USA. You can reach us at Antipather@proton.me.

2. What data we collect

You can use AntiPath without creating an account or signing in. By default, the only information the app stores is saved locally on your device:

  • Bookmarks — opportunities, scholarships, courses, or saved articles. Stored on your device, and synced to your account only if you choose to sign in (see below).
  • Display preference — whether you have enabled night mode. Stored on your device.
  • Plan selection and notification preference — which subscription tier you chose and whether you turned on Monday live-opportunity reminders. Stored on your device.

If you do not sign in, this data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

2a. Optional sign-in and account sync

AntiPath offers an optional Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google so you can sync your saved opportunities and plan across your devices. Signing in is voluntary — you can tap "Skip" and use the full app without an account.

If you choose to sign in, we receive and store a limited set of information solely to identify your account and sync your data:

  • A unique account identifier provided by Apple or Google.
  • Your email address (with Sign in with Apple you may use Apple's Hide My Email private relay address instead of your real one).
  • Your name, if your provider shares it.

We use this information only to create your account, sync your saved items and preferences across your devices, and contact you about your account if needed. We do not use it for advertising and we never sell it. You can sign out and clear this association at any time using the "Unsubscribe and sign out" option in the About tab; you may also request deletion of your synced data by contacting us.

3. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name or email address, unless you choose to sign in with Apple or Google (see Section 2a)
  • Your phone number or any other contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Payment information — AntiPath is available through a monthly subscription, offered in two tiers (Explorer at $10/month and Globetrotter at $25/month), purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play. All payment and billing processing is handled entirely by Apple and Google. AntiPath never sees, receives, or stores any payment or billing details.

4. External links

AntiPath displays listings that link directly to third-party websites — including scholarship programs, fellowship organizations, course providers, and freelance platforms. When you tap a link, you leave AntiPath and visit that third party's website or app.

We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting any personal information to them.

5. Children's privacy

AntiPath is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We collect personal information only from users who choose to sign in, and we do not knowingly allow children under 13 to create a synced account. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Antipather@proton.me and we will address your concerns promptly.

6. Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If you choose to sign in, we use Apple's Sign in with Apple and Google's Sign-In services to authenticate you; their handling of your information is governed by their own privacy policies. The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store; Apple and Google may also collect standard platform-level data (such as crash logs or download statistics) subject to their own privacy policies.

7. Data security

If you do not sign in, no personal data is transmitted to or stored by us, and the data on your device (bookmarks, preferences) is protected by your device's own security model. If you choose to sign in, the limited account information described in Section 2a is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored using reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. Your rights

If you have not signed in, we hold no personal data about you, so most data-rights requests do not apply — you can delete everything by uninstalling the app. If you have signed in:

  • You can sign out and clear the account association at any time using "Unsubscribe and sign out" in the About tab.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your synced account data by contacting us at Antipather@proton.me.
  • If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights regarding your personal data; contact us to exercise them.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy — for example, if a future version of AntiPath adds new features that involve data collection — we will update the effective date above and, where required by law, notify you through the app or the store listing. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
