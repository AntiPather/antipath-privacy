Managing or Cancelling a Subscription

AntiPath subscriptions (Explorer and Globetrotter) are billed through your Apple ID, so they're managed directly in your iPhone's Settings app, not in AntiPath:

  • Open Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions
  • Select AntiPath to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel

Cancelling stops future renewals but keeps your current plan active until the end of the billing period you already paid for.

Restoring a Purchase

If you've reinstalled AntiPath or switched devices and your subscription isn't showing up, open AntiPath and look for a Restore Purchases option in the account or paywall screen. This re-links your existing subscription to your Apple ID without charging you again.

Syncing Your Account Across Devices

Signing in with Apple or Google lets your saved bookmarks and subscription status follow you between devices. If sync isn't working, try signing out and back in, and confirm you're using the same Apple or Google account on both devices.

Antipather (the Guided Advisor)

Antipather is an on-device tool available to Globetrotter subscribers — it runs entirely on your phone and doesn't send your answers anywhere. If it isn't responding as expected, try closing and reopening AntiPath.
